The Power of Deep Learning in Your Pocket

We use computer vision, deep learning, and artificial intelligence to find available parking spaces near you. Reduce your carbon footprint by driving directly to an available parking space, instead of wasting fuel driving around your neighbourhood.

Paving The Way for Equal Access

We detect when an unauthorised car uses a disabled parking space, and alerts those concerned. We believe in equal access for all.
